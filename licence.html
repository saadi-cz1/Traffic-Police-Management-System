DRIVING LICENSE PROCEDURE

LEARNER PERMIT

  • Valid CNIC (Min 18 Year age)
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, from a designated bank.
  • Applicant has to visit in person to the License branch
  • Personal data entry along with picture
  • Learner will be issued which will mature in 45 days.

FRESH DRIVING LICENSE

  • After 45 days of issuance of learner the applicant has to visit again Driving License Branch
  • Valid CNIC
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, for a Driving Test.
  • An applicant has to pass the Traffic signs test and physical driving test.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card (Validity 5 Years).
  • An applicant can collect their Driving License Card after seven (07) days.

CONVERSION OF DRIVING LICENSE

  • Valid CNIC
  • Previous Driving License (MotorCycle / Motor Car + Jeep) in Original
  • NOC from the authority who issued the driving license.
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card (Validity 5 Years).

DUPLICATE DRIVING LICENSE

  • Valid CNIC
  • Roznamcha Report from Police Station.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card

ENDORSEMENT OF DRIVING LICENSE

  • Previous Driving License
  • Valid CNIC
  • Learner permit of at least 45 Days.
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, for Driving Test.
  • An applicant shall pass the traffic signs test and physical driving tests.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card.

RENEWAL OF EXPIRED DRIVING LICENSE

  • Original previous Driving License
  • Valid CNIC
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Fee Voucher for Driving License Card of Rs. 1000 within one month of expiry
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1500 after one month of expiry.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 3000 after 3 Years of expiry.

INTERNATIONAL DRIVING PERMIT

  • Valid Driving License of the concerned District
  • Valid CNIC
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Valid Passport
  • Valid Visa
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 5000, for an International Driving License card (validity 1 Year).

DRIVING LICENSE FOR AFGHAN NATIONAL

LEARNER PERMIT

  • Valid Afghan Citizen Card (issued by NADRA)
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Tenant Information Form (TIF) of concerned police station along with CNIC copy of house owner
  • Copy of NOC from Afghan Refugees Commissionerate (ARC).
  • Copy of NOC from Afghan Refugees Commissionerate (ARC).

DRIVING LICENSE

  • Learner Permit of at least 45 Days
  • Valid Afghan Citizen Card (issued by NADRA)
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner)
  • Tenant Information Form (TIF) of concerned police station along with CNIC copy of house owner
  • Original NOC from Afghan Refugees Commissionerate (ARC).
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, for the Driving Test
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card (validity 1 Year).

DRIVING LICENSE FOR DIPLOMATS AND EMBASSY OFFICERS

LEARNER PERMIT

  • Letter from Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) along with letter from the embassy addressed to the concerned DPO/CTO (MLAs) for issuance/renewal of driving license.
  • Photocopy of diplomate / Non-diplomatic card issued from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA).
  • Photocopy of Passport
  • Visa valid for 6 months on the date of applying for the Driving License process.
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Original passport will also be produced at the time of the process.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, from a designated bank

DRIVING LICENSE

  • Learner Permit of at least 45 Days
  • Letter from Ministry of Foreign Affairs along with letter of embassy addressed to the concerned DPO/CTO (MLAs) for issuance/renewal of driving license.
  • Photocopy of diplomate / Non-diplomatic card issued from MOFA.
  • Photocopy of Passport
  • Visa valid for 6 months on the date of applying for the Driving License process.
  • Medical Certificate (by Authorized Medical Practitioner).
  • Original passport will also be produced at the time of the process.
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 250, for the Driving Test
  • Fee Voucher of Rs. 1000, for a Driving License card
